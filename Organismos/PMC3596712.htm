Abstract
The hippo (Hpo) signaling pathway plays critical role regulation organ size. The kinase
cascade ultimately antagonizes transcriptional co-activator Yki/YAP, key regulator of
cell proliferation apoptosis. In study, performed knocking study using antisense
morpholino (MO) reagents zebrafish YAP, key transcriptional co-activator Hpo
pathway, plays critical role early embryonic development. At cellular level, yap inhibition
increases apoptosis decreases cell proliferation. Reduction yap function severely delays
developmental events, including gastrulation, cardiogenesis hematopoiesis. Knock-
yap showed evidence ventralization, including reduction dorsally expressed
marker goosecoid (gsc), expansion ventral marker gata2, disruption somites, reduction
head size. Finally, performed preliminary analysis real-time polymerase chain reaction
(qPCR) candidate targets zebrafish Hpo pathway. In conclusion, results revealed that
zebrafish yap coordinately regulates cell proliferation apoptosis required dorso-
ventral axis formation, gastrulation, cardiogenesis, hematopoiesis, somitogenesis.
Key words: Yes-Associated Protein, Yap, Zebrafish
