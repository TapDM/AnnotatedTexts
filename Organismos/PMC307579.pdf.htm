well defined for residues 12–80, with a mean backbone rms
deviation of 0.6 Å (Fig. 4a). Although proteolysis and hetero-
nuclear NOE data indicate that residues 6–85 are structured,

the oligonucleotide兾oligosaccharide-binding fold (OB-fold) (35,

␤5 form a three-stranded ␤-sheet. The core of the protein is well
