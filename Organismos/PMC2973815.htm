temperature oxygen levels, DNA damage, alternative nutrient sources. During adaptation, bacteria develop
beneficial mutations confer increased fitness new environment. Adaptation loss major non-essential
gene product cripples growth, however, studied whole-genome level. We investigated ability

replicates E. coli lacking pgi 50 days glucose M9 minimal medium characterizing endpoint clones through

increased approximately 3-fold 50-day period; 2) mutations arose adaptation, frequently in
NADH/NADPH transhydrogenases udhA pntAB stress-associated sigma factor rpoS; 3) despite similar
growth rates, distinct endpoint phenotypes developed defined different rates acetate formate
secretion. These results demonstrate E. coli adapt loss major metabolic gene product handful
mutations adaptation result multiple, alternative phenotypes.
