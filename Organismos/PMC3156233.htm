Abstract
Background: Aging fundamental biological process. Characterization genetic environmental factors that
influence lifespan crucial step understanding mechanisms aging organism level. To capture the
different effects genetic environmental factors lifespan, appropriate statistical analyses needed.

Methodology/Principal Findings: We developed online application survival analysis (OASIS) helps conduct
various novel statistical tasks involved analyzing survival data user-friendly manner. OASIS provides standard survival
analysis results including Kaplan-Meier estimates mean/median survival time taking censored survival data. OASIS
provides various statistical tests including comparison mean survival time, overall survival curve, survival rate at
specific time point. To visualize survival data, OASIS generates survival log cumulative hazard plots enable
researchers easily interpret experimental results. Furthermore, provide statistical methods analyze
variances survival datasets. In addition, users analyze proportional effects risk factors survival.

Conclusions/Significance: OASIS provides platform essential facilitate efficient statistical analyses survival data
field aging research. Web application detailed description algorithms accessible http://sbi.
postech.ac.kr/oasis.
