Abstract
The Paf complex Opisthokonts plants contains subunits: Paf1, Cdc73, Rtf1, Ctr9, Leo1. Mutations in,
loss Paf complex subunits shown cause defects histone modification, mRNA polyadenylation, and
transcription RNA polymerase I RNA polymerase II. We investigated trypanosome CTR9, essential for
trypanosome survival. The results tandem affinity purification suggested trypanosome CTR9 associates with
homologues Leo1 Cdc73; genes encoding homologues Rtf1 Paf1 found. RNAi targeting CTR9
resulted ten-fold decreases 131 essential mRNAs: included required gene expression
control, encoding subunits RNA polymerases, exoribonucleases target mRNA, RNA helicases
RNA-binding proteins. Simultaneously, genes regions subject chromatin silencing derepressed,
possibly secondary effect loss proteins required silencing, ISWI NLP1.
