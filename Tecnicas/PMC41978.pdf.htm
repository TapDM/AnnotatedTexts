The present work shows that excess SecY is inhibitory to
protein export. However, some other alleles of ftsH, an ATP-
binding site mutation and the chromosomal disruption offtsH,
cause defects of protein export without involving any overex-
pression of secY (16, 17). Therefore, FtsH may be important
not only for scavenging excess and free SecY molecules but
