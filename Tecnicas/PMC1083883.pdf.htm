In the yeast genome we have identified a good candidate for
this Man8GlcNAc2-specific lectin (Htm1p) that is homologous to

deleted for HTM1 are defective in the degradation of a mutant

protein is the putative Man8GlcNAc2-binding lectin.

formation by processing, respectively, and stabilize misfolded
CPY* (Jakob et al., 1998). Equal numbers of cells were inocu-
